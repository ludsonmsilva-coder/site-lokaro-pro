Questions

Frequently asked

Is the free plan really free?

Yes. Up to 2 units, forever, with bookings, leases, automatic invoices and email reminders included. No credit card, no trial countdown.

Does it work for rentals AND coworking at the same time?

Yes — that is the whole point. A unit can be a meeting room booked by the hour or a house with a monthly lease. Both live in the same dashboard and the same billing.

How do the automatic rent invoices work?

When you create a lease, you set the monthly value and the due day. Lokaro generates the invoice 7 days before it is due, every month, and emails the tenant a reminder 3 days before. You just watch them get paid.

What languages and platforms are supported?

English and Portuguese, light and dark mode. Lokaro launches as a mobile app for Android and iOS, with the web version available for desktops.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Downgrade or cancel whenever you want — your data stays and your account simply returns to the Free plan limits.
